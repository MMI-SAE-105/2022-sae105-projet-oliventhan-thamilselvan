About us

This site was created as a pedagogical project by Oliventhan Thamilselvan. I am a first-year student in the MMI (Multimedia and Internet) department of the IUT of Belfort-Montbéliard. This project is called "Notes for later".


I am talking about a genocide which happened in Sri Lanka in 2009. This genocide is not very popular and nobody is aware about it. It is important to remember what happened and most importantly never repeat what happened in the past.
